### Accessibility

- Pagination gets an `aria-label` provided by the `aria-label` property input.
- When Left Chevron has focus, **Enter** goes back a page, and emits the `pageChange` event.
- When Right Chevron has focus, **Enter** goes forward a page, and emits the `pageChange` event.
